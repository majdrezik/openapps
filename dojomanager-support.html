Common questions

How do I log in?

Use the phone number or email and password your coach gave you. If you're new, tap Have an invite code? on the login screen and enter the code from your coach.

I forgot my password

Tap Forgot password? on the login screen and follow the steps with the reset code from your coach.

Attendance / QR scan doesn't work

Scanning opens 1 hour before class until training ends. Ask your coach to generate a fresh QR code near class time.

Schedule or payments look wrong

Your coach updates the schedule and payments. Contact your coach first; they can fix it in the coach dashboard.
